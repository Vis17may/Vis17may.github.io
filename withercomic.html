Wither: A Minecraft Story

Life isn't fair. Wilson Stone learned that the hard way. After he invents a deadly poison. he causes a skeleton epidemic. In an accidental trip to the nether, he gets blessed by the god Herobrine. As he goes on a quest to find the powerful nether star, he gets revenge on the overworld. Will an unlikely hero rise to defeat him, or will the Wither's rampage parch the world of life...
